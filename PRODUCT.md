# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mansour (VibeCoder) — a developer building and using this as a personal task manager. Uses it daily to track work, learning tasks, and personal todos. Also doubles as a portfolio piece shown to other developers.

## Product Purpose

A lightweight personal task manager. The primary job on any screen is adding, triaging, and checking off tasks without friction. Success looks like: you open it, do the thing, close it.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, warm, and self-assured. Like Notion or Todoist — approachable without being childish, polished without being cold. Three words: **warm, focused, crafted**.

## Anti-references

- **Jira**: no enterprise density, no information overload, no bureaucratic chrome
- **Trello**: no kanban chaos, no colorful card grids competing for attention
- **Google Tasks**: no bare utilitarian feel, no zero-personality design
- **Generic SaaS templates**: no hero-metrics, no gradient cards, no startup-kit aesthetic

## Design Principles

1. **Personal, not enterprise** — one person uses this; every interaction should feel intimate and lightweight, never administrative.
2. **Warmth earns trust** — friendly and approachable means small details matter: hover states, transitions, empty states. Don't be clinical.
3. **Confidence through restraint** — personality comes from craft and proportion, not from visual noise or decoration.
4. **Earn every pixel** — no element exists for decoration alone; each one serves a task or aids comprehension.
5. **Portfolio-worthy** — this is also a showcase; every detail should make the builder proud to show it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Basic usability. No formal WCAG target, but sufficient contrast, clear focus states, and no reliance on color alone to convey meaning.
